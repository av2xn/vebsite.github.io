Your Device, Your Rules

An open, minimal, Arch-style mobile OS built for those who want complete control.

Features

Tixos is crafted to be minimal and blazing fast. Root access by default, systemd-powered init, full ADB control, and no bloat. It's not just a ROM, it's a philosophy.

Supported Devices

We currently support a growing list of devices including the Samsung Galaxy Tab A9+ (SM-X210), Note 4 (SM-N910C), and more coming soon.
